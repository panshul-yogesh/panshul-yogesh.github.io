Our Mission Since 1999

The Society for Technical Communication India brings together the most talented technical writers from across India and internationally. As a not-for-profit organization, we facilitate the sharing of information about trends, standards, best practices, and experiences within the industry.

As a learning organization, we continuously strive to organize impactful learning sessions and provide a collaborative platform for the technical communication community in India to thrive and grow together.
